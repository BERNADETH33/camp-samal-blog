Escape to Camp Samal

Nestled in the rolling hills of Barangay Arcon, Tumauini, Camp Samal is a premier eco-adventure park and nature retreat. Known for its refreshing landscapes, breathtaking scenic views, and abundance of outdoor fun, it’s a favorite destination for families, adventurers, and anyone seeking peace and serenity in nature.

Essence:

Camp Samal is Tumauini’s green sanctuary — a place where adventure meets serenity. Whether you’re seeking fun, reflection, or a much-needed break from city life, this nature escape offers the perfect retreat in the heart of Isabela.

The Camp Samal Experience

Nature & Relaxation

  • Scenic hiking trails
  • Kayaking on the lake
  • Lounging by the pool with a panoramic view of the Sierra Madre mountain range.

Adventure & Eco-Activities

  • The exciting zip-line experience.
